ABOUT

Hella is a casual bar for everyone. We're all about doing what we love, making exciting cocktails, hosting unique events, and creating good vibes. Our mission? Bringing cocktails to people in a laid-back way.

BAR STUFF

At Hella, we offer a wide range of delicious cocktails, focusing on exciting flavors and quality. Whether you're craving something fruity and pink or leaning towards the bitter side, we've got you covered. Come on in and treat yourself to a Hella-nice experience!
