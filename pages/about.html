ABOUT

ATMOSPHERE

Hella is a bar in nice surroundings with a calm and cozy atmosphere placed at Aarhus Ø.

With soft lighting, comfortable seating and cozy energy guests are invited to enjoy a cozy evening with friends.

We always set the mood with our vinyl records, exploring a wide range of genres.

Feel free to choose one and set the vibe! Book a table here.

THE TEAM

We are three guys who have a passion for making good cocktails.

We are always here to give you recommendations from our drinks menu or answer any questions you might have.

If you would like to hang out with us, we occasionally host themed events where you can enjoy authentic food, refreshing cocktails and good music.
