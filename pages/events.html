DISCOVER OUR EVENTS

At Hella, we are passionate about having fun and creating unique nights. This is why we often team up with friends and acquaintances to host themed events, featuring delicious food, DJ sets and cocktails, ensuring an unforgettable night filled with good vibes.

We are open for walk-ins and bookings, but if you want to secure your spot, book a table here.

EVENTS

BALKAN NIGHT WITH PARTIZANI

OPEN FOR WALK-INS AND BOOKING

On the “longest Friday” Easter Friday the 29th we are joined by our good friends from Partizani Aarhus and Balkan tunes being produced by @papaskrald.

We are serving small ice cold tap beer and experimental Easter Slivovitz cocktail as long as the lam is rotating or sold out.

BALKAN NIGHT

TACO NIGHT WITH AGAVE BY MONTEL

OPEN FOR WALK-INS AND BOOKING

Join us Friday, April 26th, for a special visit from Agave By Montiel and the finest soulful Cali tunes from @djfmd.

Enjoy crispy tostada-style tacos with or without meat until sold out.

Rosa Montiel brings a great selection of mezcal while the bar is making margarita and Paloma’s by the glass or pitcher.

TACO NIGHT

FRIED CHICKEN NIGHT WITH CAPO

OPEN FOR WALK-INS AND BOOKING

Join us Friday, April 24th, for a special visit by CAPO and some semi-angry hip-hop tunes played by the duo @madsappeal and @djfmd.

Enjoy a mouthwatering fried chicken sandwich, with buffalo sauce and dill pickles.

In the bar, we will be focusing in American whisky bourbon cocktails, one-slip martinis and pickle backs with mezcal.
